About me

I was born on the 1st of March 1994 in the capital of Ukraine - Kiev. In 2016 I moved to Strasbourg in order to continue my studies in the field of web, language engineering and translation (Caweb) at the University of Strasbourg. As a person, I have always been very ambitious. Life is like riding a bicycle, to maintain balance, you must move. Always keeping in mind this well-known statement I have constantly challenged myself with different types of complicated but at the same time interesting tasks that tempered me and as a result contributed to my personal growth and new acheivements. I believe that we are the ones who decide everything about our lives. For me, planning and organization are the most important qualities that help me to achieve my goals. Now, holding two higher educations I feel I have chosen the right way and that's why now I am here, doing what I like and hoping to achieve my own Everest ! I invite you to get acquainted with my personality in a more detailed way and take a look at the most important accomplishments in my life as well as my educational background and career path.
